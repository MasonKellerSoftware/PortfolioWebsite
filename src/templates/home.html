About

About Me

My name is Mason Keller, and I am currently a rising Senior majoring in Computer Science at Auburn University. I know every resume and portfolio website says that they have a passion for coding, but I genuinely do. I have been coding in some shape or form since second grade, where we were introduced to the website Scratch. While most kids just completed the lessons, I eventually started to create my own projects and games. I have always loved the idea of creating something from nothing, and coding allows me to do just that. As I grew older, I moved into text based languages, at the time primarily Python and Java. My senior year of high school I had the opportunity to do independent research at the University of Alabama, where I researched and developed a program to demonstrate the potential and actual benefits of computer science in the field of automotive safety. I didn't expect to recieve any recognition for my work, but I ended up being first honorable mention in the state. I would eventually recieve the department award for Computer Science at my high school

About this website

The idea for this website came about when applying for internships. I had been told multiple times that I needed to have projects that demonstrate my abilities and skills. The most common suggestion I receieved was to create a portfolio website. The idea seemed daunting at the time, I had never seriously touched web development before. However, upon doing some research I found out that there were multitudes of frameworks and tools that made web dev much simplier than I had imagined. This website is built primarily with Flask, a lightweight Python web framework, and utilizes Bootstrap for responsive design. I went with Flask because Python is one of the easiest languages to write and learn new concepts with, and I went with Bootstrap because the sheer amount of resources available on the framework. While it is definitely not the most complex or visually impressive, I still am proud of the work I put into it. It has taught me a lot about web development, and I hope to continue to improve both my skills and this website in the future.
